Developers that want to contribute to the ScalaLab effort by writing Scala higher level interfaces to those libraries are welcomed in order to produce both research work and an even better scientific programming environment.

If someone wants to contribute, don't hesitate to contact me.



Sorry, if I forgot some library, please remind me to add it!



Again, thanks to the many contributors of the ScalaLab components and of course to the many creators of the powerful JVM and Scala language infrastructure.
